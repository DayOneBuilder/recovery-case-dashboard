# Recovery Lead Email Template

Use this template only after the case data has a source-backed `recoveryOpportunity`, a `contact`, and an `outreachQueue` item.

```text
Subject: Public-source recovery lead: [CASE] / [LEAD]

Hello [TEAM] team,

I am sharing a public-source recovery lead related to [CASE].

I am an independent researcher acting in good faith. I am not requesting access to funds, private systems, non-public victim data, or account information. The goal is to help route actionable recovery intelligence to the right team.

Current lead:

- Opportunity: [OPPORTUNITY]
- Value at stake: [VALUE]
- Current ask: [ASK]
- Evidence packet: [PACKET LINK]
- Public dashboard: [DASHBOARD LINK]

Why I am contacting you now:

[WHY NOW]

What I am asking for:

1. Please confirm whether this is the correct route for this recovery intelligence.
2. Please assign a case/reference ID if this should be tracked formally.
3. Please confirm whether the described funds or venue branch are already frozen, preserved, settled, or otherwise handled.
4. Please tell me what additional public-source evidence would be useful and safe to provide.

Reward / attribution position:

I am providing the lead in good faith and value-first. If information from this investigation materially contributes to asset recovery, preservation, enforcement action, or identification of responsible parties, I would appreciate being considered for standard researcher credit and any applicable recovery, bounty, or intelligence reward process.

I will avoid publishing sensitive operational details while your team reviews.

Best regards,
[YOUR NAME]
[YOUR CONTACT]
```
